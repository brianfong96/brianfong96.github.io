About Me

Senior software engineer with a passion for building across disciplines.

I'm Brian Fong, a senior software engineer. My work spans mixed reality, game development, cybersecurity, red teaming, and AI. I enjoy taking on hard problems across different domains and building tools and experiences that actually get used.

Mixed Reality

I started my software engineering career as an intern at Microsoft, where I got the chance to work on the HoloLens. I helped build a mixed reality application in partnership with BC Cancer to support cancer research.

Game Development

Game dev is a hobby I like to pursue in my free time. I enjoy prototyping interactive experiences, experimenting with mechanics, and building things just for the fun of it.

Cybersecurity & Red Teaming

This is where I started my full-time software engineering career. I worked at Microsoft on the Azure Red Team and then the Microsoft Red Team, where I helped build a graph of cloud assets to identify attack paths. I developed a system to programmatically identify all attack paths that violate security boundaries.

AI

This is where I am right now — I'm currently working on M365 Copilot at Microsoft, building AI-powered experiences that help people get more done.
