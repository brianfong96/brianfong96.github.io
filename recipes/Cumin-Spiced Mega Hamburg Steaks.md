This recipe creates a high-protein, flavorful meatloaf or "Mega Hamburg Steak" using a panade of whole wheat bread and milk to ensure 3kg of ground beef remains tender and juicy. Seasoned with cumin, cayenne, and powdered chicken stock, these portions provide a savory, slightly spicy profile perfect for a hearty main course. By shaping the beef into six massive 500g patties, you achieve a roasted exterior with a succulent center, bridging the gap between a traditional meatloaf and a Japanese-style Hamburg steak.

### Ingredients
* **3kg** Ground Beef (Medium or Lean)
* **6-8 slices** Whole Wheat Bread (torn into small bits)
* **1 ½ cups** Whole Milk
* **3 large** Eggs (beaten)
* **4 tbsp** Powdered Chicken Stock
* **2 tbsp** Cumin
* **1 tsp** Cayenne Pepper
* **2 tbsp** Red Pepper flakes
* **1 large** Onion (finely minced)
* **4 cloves** Garlic (minced)

---

### Preparation Instructions
1.  **Prepare the Panade:** In a large mixing bowl, soak the torn whole wheat bread in the milk for 10 minutes. Use a fork to mash the bread into a consistent paste. This step is essential for moisture retention.
2.  **Incorporate Seasonings:** Stir the beaten eggs, powdered chicken stock, cumin, cayenne, red pepper, minced onion, and garlic directly into the bread paste. Mixing the spices into the liquid base ensures even distribution throughout the massive 3kg of meat.
3.  **Combine with Beef:** Add the ground beef to the bowl. Using your hands, gently fold the meat into the seasoned mixture. Stop as soon as the ingredients are combined; overworking the beef will result in a tough, rubbery texture.
4.  **Shape the Steaks:** Divide the mixture into six equal portions, roughly 500g each. Shape them into thick, oval patties. Use your thumb to press a deep, wide indentation into the center of each patty to prevent them from doming or bulging as they cook.
5.  **Arrange for Roasting:** Place the patties on two parchment-lined baking sheets (three per sheet) to allow for proper air circulation.

---

### Oven Roasting & Temperature
* **Oven Temperature:** Pre-heat to **190°C (375°F)**.
* **Roasting Time:** Cook for **35–45 minutes**.
* **Target Internal Temperature:** Pull the patties from the oven when they reach **68°C (155°F)**.
* **Resting:** Let the patties rest on the baking sheet for **10–15 minutes** before serving. Carry-over cooking will bring the final internal temperature to the safe mark of **71°C (160°F)** while allowing the juices to redistribute.
