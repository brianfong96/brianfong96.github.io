Recipe

Cumin-Spiced Mega Hamburg Steaks

Click any section to expand. Click again to collapse.

Back to Recipes

Overview

click to explore

High-protein, savory "Mega Hamburg Steaks" using a panade of whole wheat bread and milk to keep 3kg of ground beef tender. Seasoned with cumin, cayenne, and chicken stock — six massive 500g patties bridging meatloaf and Japanese Hamburg steak.

10 Items

Ingredients

click to explore
  • 3 kg Ground Beef (Medium or Lean)
  • 6-8 slices Whole Wheat Bread (torn)
  • 1 1/2 cups Whole Milk
  • 3 large Eggs (beaten)
  • 4 tbsp Powdered Chicken Stock
  • 2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 large Onion (finely minced)
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
5 Steps

Preparation

click to explore
  1. Panade: Soak torn bread in milk 10 min, mash to paste.
  2. Season: Stir eggs, chicken stock, cumin, cayenne, red pepper, onion, garlic into paste.
  3. Combine: Fold beef into mixture gently. Don't overwork.
  4. Shape: Divide into 6 x 500g oval patties. Thumb-press center indentation.
  5. Arrange: Place on 2 parchment-lined sheets, 3 per sheet.
Oven

Roasting

click to explore
Oven 190C / 375F
Time 35-45 min
Pull at 68C / 155F
Rest 10-15 min Carry-over to 71C
Tips

Key Tips

click to explore
  • The panade (bread + milk paste) is essential for moisture retention in this much meat.
  • Mix spices into the liquid base first for even distribution.
  • Stop folding as soon as combined — overworked beef = tough texture.
  • The thumb indentation prevents doming during roasting.
  • Resting lets carry-over cooking finish the job safely.
