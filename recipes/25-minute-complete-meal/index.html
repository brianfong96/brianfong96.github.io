🥩🍝🥦 Quick Steak, Pasta & Broccolini

Air fryer steak, Instant Pot garlic parmesan pasta and stovetop broccolini cooked in sync.

Stage 1

🔪 Prep

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye or NY strip steaks
  • 8 oz penne or rigatoni
  • 2 tbsp butter, 5–6 garlic cloves
  • 2½ cups broth, 1 cup cream, ¾ cup Parmesan
  • 1 bunch broccolini
