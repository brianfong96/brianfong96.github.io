🥩🍝🥦 Quick Steak, Pasta & Broccolini

Air fryer steak, Instant Pot garlic parmesan pasta and stovetop broccolini cooked in sync.

Step 1

📋 Plan & Prep

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye or NY strip steaks
  • 8 oz penne or rigatoni
  • 2 tbsp butter, 5–6 garlic cloves
  • 2½ cups broth, 1 cup cream, ¾ cup Parmesan
  • 1 bunch broccolini

Steak Doneness Guide (400°F):

DonenessPullFinalTime
Rare120–125°F125–130°F8–9 min
Med Rare125–130°F130–135°F9–10 min
Medium135–140°F140–145°F10–11 min
Med Well140–145°F145–150°F11–12 min
Well150–155°F155–160°F12–13 min
Step 2

🍝 Start Pasta

  • Set Instant Pot to sauté. Melt butter and cook garlic for 1 min.
  • Add broth, cream, pasta and seasonings.
  • Seal and pressure cook on High for 5 min.
Step 3

🥩 Air Fry Steaks

  • Preheat air fryer to 400°F.
  • Lightly oil steaks and season with salt and pepper.
  • Air fry 9–13 min, flipping halfway. Rest 5 min.
Step 4

🥦 Steam Broccolini

  • Bring ½ inch salted water to a boil in a pan.
  • Add broccolini, cover and steam 3–4 min until bright green.
  • Drain and toss with olive oil or lemon.
Step 5

🍽️ Finish & Serve

  • Quick release the Instant Pot and stir in Parmesan.
  • Let pasta thicken a couple minutes.
  • Plate steak with pasta and broccolini.
